About

The purpose of this project is to produce an analytical model that displays parameters to complement decision making when it comes to investing in home real estate. It evaluates a city's rent ratio traits and monitors the response or the trigger for rent ratio movement, driven by average city home values and home sales counts. The idea is for real estate investors to be ahead of the gentrification process of a metropolitan area of interest, before it actually has happened or in its beginning phases. Getting the information early will ultimately lead to higher ROIs and sound real estate investing.
